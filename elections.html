Elections 2017

The elections in March 2017 will be held to determine the distribution of the 150 seats in the Tweede Kamer over all political parties. The seats in the Eerste Kamer will be distributed according to the distribution of the seats in the Tweede Kamer.

Voting

Voting is done at voting booths which can be found at numerous places in all cities.

Current distribution

VVD

The VVD is currently the leading party and has 40 seats.

PvdA

The PvdA currently has 35 seats.

SP

The SP currently has 15 seats.

CDA

The CDA currently has 13 seats.

D66

D66 currently has 12 seats.

PVV

The PVV currently has 12 seats.

ChristenUnie

The ChristenUnie currently has 5 seats.

GroenLinks

GroenLinks currently has 4 seats.

SGP

The SGP currently has 3 seats.

PvdD

The PvdD currently has 2 seats.

50plus

50plus currently has 1 seat.

Remainder

There are 8 remaining seats. These are distributed among members of the Tweede Kamer that have left their party but still wish to be a member.
